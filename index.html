About this guide

Understanding the Brain as It Ages

✦

Neurodegenerative diseases affect tens of millions of people worldwide, yet many of us know very little about how they work.

Whether you are a caregiver, a student, an older adult thinking about brain health, or simply curious, this guide is for you. Each section uses plain language, clear visuals, and defined terms to make complex science approachable.

55M+ People living with dementia worldwide
10M New dementia diagnoses per year
10M Living with Parkinson's globally
~30K Americans living with ALS

Sources: World Health Organization, Alzheimer's Association, ALS Association (2024–2025 estimates)
